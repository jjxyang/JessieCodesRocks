First Iteration

This design was the first iteration of the final design. I decided that the pattern being cut off at the edges of the circle looked unappealing and unnatural, so I re-organized the icons into a circle, laying them out with copy-paste and adjusting by hand. I also changed the font because this font appears far more complex and formal than the surrounding icons.


Alternate Design 1

This design was created by making an Illustrator pattern swatch out of the icons and creating a rectangle filled by said swatch. However, I decided this square, since it gets cut off, wouldn't suit a T-shirt very well.


Alternate Design 2

I am very partial to this alternate design because it has a very clean layout for all of the icons and is as neat as I designed each icon to be. It makes a solid alternative, depending on if you want the final version's energy or this version's minimalism.
